RAInfall builds 3D ionizing radiation acoustic imaging

Product Description

RAInfall is a radiological imaging system for monitoring cancer treatments. The medical device enables complex 3D mapping of the radiation dose, allowing medical professionals to better quantify and adjust radiation dose, enabling the future of adaptive treatment. Since RAInfall is a monitoring system which builds on existing cancer treatments, it does not introduce additional side effects, although there are a litany of severe side effects already associated with cancer treatment. If you are concerned about cancer treatment side effects, you should consult your physician, because they are different for different treatment plans. RAInfall is treatment plan agnostic, as long as the plan involves radiative therapy. RAInfall is not a combination product, with its primary mode of action being a medical device monitoring radiation dosage. Because this device is only to be used by medical professionals in controlled environments, and it has the capacity to be lethal if it inaccurately measures the progress of radiative treatment, it is regulated as a class III device by the FDA.

Target Demographic

RAInfall is designed for use by all cancer patients receiving radiation therapy. It has been tested on animals, and will be tested on humans. While RAInfall will be mostly marketed for and used on human patients, it could be used for animal patients as well. This technology is needed to enhance existing cancer treatments; more monitoring allows for more adjustment and safer dosage. This technology is also useful for the development of future, highly adaptive, personalized cancer treatments.
