iRAI

Ionizing radiation acoustic imaging (iRAI) is a noninvasive imaging technique which uses the radiation emitted during radiological cancer treatment to reconstruct the dosage after it passes through the patient. iRAI can use that data in conjunction with the treatment plan to analyze how much radiation was absorbed by the body. Our advanced system performs this checking in 3 dimensions, constructing a map of radiation absorption for your physician to analyze. This system is a diagnostic tool, but it will allow cancer treatment to be fit to every patient individually, resulting in more effective treatments.
